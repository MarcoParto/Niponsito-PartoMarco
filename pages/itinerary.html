ITINERARY

Depending On The Day You Can Find Us In Different Parks Of The City

Enjoy Eating Surrounded By Nature

TUESDAY & THURSDAY

"3 de Febrero"

You can find us in the iconic and beautiful park "3 de febrero" every Tuesday and Thursday from 18:00 to 23:00 hrs. We are not fixed in one place, so you will have to wander through the trees to find us! Look for us and enjoy a delicious Ramen as a reward!

WEDNESDAY & FRIDAY

"El Rosedal"

Every Wednesday and Friday from 18:00 to 23:00 hrs you will find us in the emblematic park "El Rosedal". Few spectacles are as beautiful as the blooming of the almost 8,000 roses of 93 different species that grow in this park. And there is nothing like enjoying a nutritious bowl of Ramen surrounded by these roses!

SATURDAY & SUNDAY

"Plaza Sicilia"

Come to "Plaza Sicilia" every Saturday and Sunday from 18:00 to 23:00 hrs and you will find us here. In this "plaza" surrounded by Libertador, Sarmiento, Berro and Casares avenues; there is also located the Japanese Garden. Come and enjoy an adventure for all your senses!
