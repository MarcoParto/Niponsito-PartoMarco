We are a Ramen Street Cart!

We bring you one of the oldest gastronomic traditions in Japan. We are a food cart that travels through iconic points of the city to bring you the best Ramen you will ever taste. We are direct descendants of Japanese, and ramen lovers. Do you want to know more about who we are and what we do? come and visit us!
