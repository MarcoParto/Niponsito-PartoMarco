Chicken Ramen

Are you ready to indulge in an authentic feast of Asian flavors? Our Chicken Ramen will transport you to the streets of Tokyo with every slurp. Treat yourself to carefully selected ramen noodles, immersed in a homemade chicken broth that has been slow-cooked to achieve a deep and comforting flavor. Our juicy chicken breast is marinated in a secret blend of spices, giving it an irresistible taste. Additionally, we accompany this dish with a variety of fresh and crunchy vegetables, such as green onions, carrots, and bamboo shoots, to add texture and freshness.

Main Ingredients:

  • Delicious wheat ramen noodles, perfectly cooked for an ideal texture.
  • Juicy chicken breast marinated in a blend of Asian spices, adding a unique and tasty flavor.
  • Homemade chicken broth, prepared with fresh ingredients and full of authentic flavor.
  • Fresh and crunchy vegetables, for a touch of freshness in every bite.

Beef Ramen

Are you craving a rich and savory taste experience? Our Beef Ramen is a symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. Dive into a bowl filled with tender beef slices, perfectly cooked ramen noodles, and a hearty beef broth that has been simmered to perfection. Our beef is marinated in a special blend of spices, infusing each bite with mouthwatering goodness. Complementing the beef are an array of fresh vegetables, including bok choy, mushrooms, and green onions, adding texture and vibrant flavor to every spoonful.

Main Ingredients:

  • Fresh vegetables, adding a pop of color and nutrition to the dish.
